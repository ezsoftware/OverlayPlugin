OBS Solution without Streamer build
===================================

1. Open OBS.
2. on Overlay config, click 'Open DevTools'.
3. Click 'Console' or press `Esc`.
4. Type `open('.')` and press enter.
5. Now you get huge window, minimize them and configure OBS.
